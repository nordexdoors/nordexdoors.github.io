About

Welcome to Nordex Commercial Interiors / Fitouts

We specialise in a wide range of trade skills including carpentry, plastering, acoustic solutions and interior/exterior feature finishes. We have solutions for business relocation’s, office fit-outs, partitions, acoustic upgrades and timber finishes for both interiors and exteriors. With over 25 year’s industry experience, we have the expertise and the solutions for your future project.

We work closely with you to create your ideal working environment. Every business is different, so we specialize in bespoke solutions to suit your individual needs, no cookie cutter approach.
